Timesheets

Here’s to new beginnings. Azalio will track time and attendance of your employees for you.

Timesheets

  • View timesheets along with photo and location data.
  • View overtime red flags and out of schedule yellow flags with each entry.
  • Export timesheets for payroll.

Scheduler

  • Create schedules for your employees.
  • At run time, manage the store hours.
  • Employees can view their and schedules of other team members on their mobile app.

Task

  • Easily create tasks for your team.
  • Prioritize and assign tasks.
  • Use task history for communication between team members.

Task

  • Make your employees feel special.
  • Recognize your employees on a job well done.
  • Choose from a number of badges.

Organization

  • Create a database of your employees.
  • Assign them role‐based permissions.
  • For web users, enter their email address. For mobile app, enter their phone number and 4‐digit PIN.

Messaging

  • Chat with your employees anytime anywhere.
  • Send a message to all employees of any store.
  • View read receipts.
